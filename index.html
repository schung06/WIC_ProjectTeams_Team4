About the Farmer's Market

Epstein Farmer's Market is a mini food festival featuring stands from local San Diego businesses and food trucks. They feature fresh produce and students' favorite small eats such as crepes, dumplings, and matcha drinks. The market is open to all students and the public every Tuesdays from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM at Epstein Family Plaza.
